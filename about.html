ABOUT US

Our story begins more than - 70-year period of the British Mandate in Israel. Ben and Gershon, were a pair of brothers who came to Israel in 1945 and decided to put some life in the country, and began producing handmade fresh ice cream.
Rumors of the finest ice cream flavor quickly spread and demand grew for this ice cream. Cafés wanted to sell the ice cream, and all state residents wanted to purchase it. In response to the demand, the brothers decided to set up a small factory for the production of ice cream.
The plant has evolved over the years, moved to a new place, the machines become more sophisticated, and the brothers began to produce a variety of ice cream flavors and textures.
Today the factory covers an area of ​​about 5 hectares which produce a wide range of 200 products, including glaciers, ice-cream, cones, ice cream Diet, Snacks, ice cream and family packs. The products are produced under kosher and are manufactured from only the land configuration!
